Why?

Annually, the United Nations Office for the Coordination of Humanitarian Affairs (UNOCHA) releases the Global Humanitarian Overview. We saw it, and we wanted to do something about it.

What?

Our algorithm analyzes winners of the Pulitzer Prize for International Reporting (from 1995 onwards) and captures a similarity score for any article we feed it. For the time being, we use it to suggest articles for each country that the UNOCHA has a Humanitarian Response Plan for: the 21 countries you see on the home page.

What next?

We know that our algorithm is not without its limitations. Good journalism takes many forms and is not exclusive to Pulitzer-winners. We are working tirelessly to improve it.

Why?

Annually, the United Nations Office for the Coordination of Humanitarian Affairs (UNOCHA) releases the Global Humanitarian Overview. We saw it. We wanted to learn more. So we built a tool. Now we want to share that tool with you.

What?

Our algorithm analyzes winners of the Pulitzer Prize for International Reporting and captures a similarity score for any article that we feed it. We use it to suggest powerful articles about pressing humanitarian crises: to unearth journalism about problems that matter. Our database is updated daily.

Mission

Our mission is to connect people to powerful journalism. We believe in journalismâ€™s capacity to layer narratives. To call to action.
To tell untold stories.

Why?

Annually, the United Nations Office for the Coordination of Humanitarian Affairs (UNOCHA) releases the Global Humanitarian Overview. We saw it. We wanted to learn more. And so we decided to build a tool. Now we want to share that tool with you.

What?

Our algorithm analyzes winners of the Pulitzer Prize for International Reporting and captures a similarity score for any article that we feed it. Currently, we use it to suggest articles for every country that the UNOCHA has a Humanitarian Response Plan for.

What next?

Our solution is not perfect, and good journalism is certainly not exclusive to winners of the Pulitzer Prize for International Reporting.
To that end, we are actively looking for ways to improve.
